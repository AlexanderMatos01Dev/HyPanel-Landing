<p class="lead">The days of server lists are fading. Short-form video is king.</p>

## The Hook
You have 0.5 seconds. Show something crazy. An explosion, a custom mob, a funny fail.

## The CTA
"IP in bio". Make it easy. Use a custom domain (like `play.myserver.com`) that is easy to remember.
