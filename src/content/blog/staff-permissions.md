## The Principle of Least Privilege
Only give your moderators the exact permissions they need to do their job. They don't need `/stop` or `/op`.

## LuckPerms Integration
HyPanel has a built-in visual editor for LuckPerms. You can drag and drop permissions to groups without ever typing a command.
